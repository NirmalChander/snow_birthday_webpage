I am endlessly grateful to have you in my life.

You are not just beautiful—you are stunning, both inside and out. You make life feel magical, and I feel so fortunate to be a part of your journey. If there’s one thing I wish for you today, it’s to know how special you are to me.

Thank you for being the incredible person you are.

Every moment with you is like living in a beautiful dream.

You’re a treasure, a gift lucky enough to know you.

You are, and always will be, the most important person in my life.


Happy Birthday, Snow.


With all my love,

Nirmal
